Pie Crust:
2.5 cups (375 g) flour plus more for dusting
3 tbsp sugar 
0.5 tsp salt
1 cup (225 g) vegan butter cubed and chilled
4 tbsp cold water

Cinnamon Apple Filling:
2 lbs (1 kg) crispy fresh apples, don't use floury ones
1 cup raisins
0.5 cup (100 g) sugar 
2 tbsp cornstarch
1-2 tsp cinnamon
0.25 tsp nutmeg (optional)
0.5 lemon juice

Decoration:
almond flakes (optional)

To brush:
a bit of plant milk / dairy-free cream or melted coconut oil
1 tbsp brown sugar
