Easy 1-Pot Vegan Spaghetti

15 ml olive oil
160 g diced onion
3 cloves garlic, minced
227 g cremini mushrooms, thinly sliced
1 medium yellow or red bell pepper, diced
1/2 tsp sea salt
1 tsp dried oregano (or Italian seasoning blend)
1 tsp thyme
1 tsp dried tarragon
1/4 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
2 (680-g) jars favorite marinara
360 ml water
340 g dry gluten-free spaghetti 
1 big handful spinach or other green of choice (optional)
Yeast flakes (optional)
